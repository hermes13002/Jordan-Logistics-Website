ABOUT US
Jordan Logistics is involved in planning, implementing and controlling the efficient, effective flow and storage of goods, services and related information from the point of origin to the point of consumption for the purpose of satisfying our customers prerequisite. The organisation is made up of experienced and highly skilled professionals in their fields who provides the utmost services to our customers. We are also involved in freight transportation and Inventory Management.
